Welcome!
Let me instroduce myself...

I am Millie. An Experience Designer with a strong background in Health and Nutrition. Working previously as a Nutritionist gave me tons of transferable skills to my tool belt such as User Interviews, Problem solving and Human-centred design approach.

In 2022 I decided to change my life completely by taking the next step into my career change. I joined the UX Design Bootcamp at General Assembly to improve my skills as a new Designer, and also to be able to practise everything I’ve learned in the past 2 years through books and other online courses.

I love contributing to the end-to-end design process, from Research all the way to Deliver the best solution for all.
